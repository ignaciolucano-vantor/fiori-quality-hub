# Hackathon Brief

## Working Title

Ctrl+Alt+Defect

## Theme

AI for Operational Efficiency (Back-Office)

## Problem Statement

SAP Fiori UAT and issue capture are often fragmented across screenshots, chat messages, spreadsheets, and manually written tickets. This slows triage, weakens traceability, and creates rework between testers, functional teams, and developers.

## Hypothesis

A reusable Fiori Quality Hub with structured issue capture, evidence collection, AI-ready export, and automatic Jira creation can reduce the time from defect discovery to actionable engineering backlog while improving the quality of handoff.

## Demonstrable Outcome

A working prototype where a user:
- launches the framework from a Fiori app
- executes a lightweight UAT case
- captures evidence
- creates a linked issue
- generates a remediation report
- creates a Jira record automatically

## Team Profile

Recommended teammates:
- ABAP and RAP
- UI5 and Fiori
- QA and UAT
- Jira or platform integration
- product or process perspective
